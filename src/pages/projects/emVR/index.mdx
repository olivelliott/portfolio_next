## Description

emVR™ facilitates EMDR therapy sessions in VR via the web which offers an innovative and effective way to participate in immersive tele-health sessions and remote EMDR experiences. The platform is accessible from any VR headset with an internet connection. Using the web to engage in remote tele-health removes many of the barriers to accessing immersive therapy.


## Contributions

- Led the development of emVR, a groundbreaking online platform designed to enable therapists to deliver Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) therapy to clients through immersive virtual reality experiences.

- Engineered robust communication systems within emVR with Websockets to seamlessly transmit therapist settings and parameters to clients, ensuring a tailored and effective therapy experience.

- Crafted a fully immersive virtual environment that harnesses the power of visual, audio, and haptic stimulation, offering clients a highly engaging and therapeutic VR experience.
